01. About

Who I Am

I'm a Staff Software Engineer based in NYC with a passion for building clean, robust systems at scale. I thrive at the intersection of great engineering and great culture, celebrated as a servant leader who brings the best out of teams.

My career has spanned backend engineering, distributed systems, and technical leadership across companies like Automox, Datasite, and Target. I've built everything from MDM solutions and billing systems processing billions of dollars, to data pipelines ingesting petabytes of information, to infrastructure powering target.com, one of the top 60 most-visited sites in the US.

Outside of code, I'm endlessly curious about the world. I've traveled to ~80 countries, I'm fluent in Thai and English, and always learning more. I genuinely love developing people: I've coached interns and junior engineers through code, career, and everything in between, watching them grow into confident, autonomous seniors. I care just as much about the team as the individual, and I work hard to build environments where everyone does their best work. I believe the best engineers never stop learning, and I live that every day. Above all, I try to show up with kindness and thoughtfulness, in code and in life.
